A mesmerizing sculpture capturing the beauty of heritage and the strength of knowledge. This piece portrays a young girl engrossed in reading, her afro blossoming with miniature sculptures of ancestors, family, and historical figures. Each figure in her hair represents a story, a life, a piece of her history. As she reads, she connects to the wisdom, resilience, and love of those who came before her, symbolizing the depth of identity and the importance of legacy. A powerful homage to the richness of culture and the timeless journey of self-discovery.
